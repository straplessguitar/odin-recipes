Odin Recipes: Poro Snax

Description

Poros can be found all over the universe of League of Legends.
Their best source of nourishment, Poro Snax, are tasty treats of butter cookies and chocolate!

"Eat, eat and grow strong!"- Braum


Ingredients

  • 240gr icing sugar
  • 240gr butter
  • 400gr flour
  • An egg white
  • 50gr cornstarch
  • Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Wisk the butter untill it's soft and fluffy
  2. Then add the sifted icing sugar and mix
  3. Add the vanilla extract and the egg white and mix
  4. Then add the sifted all-pourpose flour and a pinch of salt
  5. Knead by hand and make a cylindrical shape
  6. Let rest in the fridge for 30 to 45 minutes
  7. Strech the dough with a roller into a rectangle shape an inch thick
  8. Spread a Nutella-like cream over the dough with a spatula
  9. Roll it up, but be careful, as to not break the dough
  10. After rolling it, wrap it in wrapping paper and leave it in the fridge for 2 hours or in the freezer for 1 hour
  11. Now that the dough is cold it'll be easier to cut into the desired shape without breaking the dough
  12. Cut slices about 1cm thick and lay them out on baking paper, separate them as they might grown in diameter
  13. Leave them in the oven for 10 to 20 minutes at 180ÂºC (depends on the oven)
  14. When you take them out let them cool off and there you go!
Serve with some Freljordian Elnuk milk! Or just regular cow milk!
